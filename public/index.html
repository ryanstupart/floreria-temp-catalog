About Floreria Florentina / Sobre Floreria Florentina

Blooming Happiness, One Bouquet at a Time

Welcome to Florería Florentina LLC, where we are committed to designing beautiful floral arrangements that are perfect for all occasions. From custom bouquets to stuffed animals, balloons, and lovely gifts, we offer a wide range of products that is sure to satisfy all of your floral needs.

Bienvenido a Florería Florentina LLC, donde nos comprometemos a diseñar hermosos arreglos florales perfectos para toda ocasión. Desde ramos personalizados hasta peluches, globos y hermosos regalos, ofrecemos una amplia variedad de productos para sus necesidades florales.

My Story / Mi Historia

Floreria Florentina started in honor of my mom, the strongest woman I know. My mother started her own small business when I was 11 years old. She began selling small items in a small location inside a farmers market, then later had the opportunity to grow her business into a bigger location.

I have been with her since I was 13 years old, and she taught me to work hard and be independent. She is the reason I started creating floral arrangements, always pushing me to be a better person and overachieve.

She learned this from her own mother, my grandma, who was also a big part of my life and helped raise me until I was 5 years old. She passed away in 2016, so I named my shop after her. She is the reason my mother is such a hard worker, and both of them taught me to achieve bigger and better.

Floreria Florentina nació en honor a mi mamá, la mujer más fuerte que conozco. Ella me enseñó a trabajar duro, ser independiente y siempre buscar algo mejor. Este negocio también honra a mi abuela, quien fue una gran parte de mi vida y cuya fuerza sigue inspirando nuestra historia.
